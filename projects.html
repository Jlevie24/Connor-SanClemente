Anchoring System

Design anchoring system for Horizontal Test Stand. Designed to withstand 3,000 lbf thrust. Finite Element Analysis (Solidworks Simulation) of Horizontal Test Stand and expected load. Hand calculation of anchoring system stresses to reinforce FEA results. Material analysis of anchoring system such as factor of safety and yield strength considerations. Present formalized CDR to other members for constructive feedback on design.

MoTE Enclosure

Modification of PCB enclosure to become integrated with PCB housed inside. CNC milling of various connector holes (RJ45, 2-pole power pole, thermocouple) using GibbsCAM. 3D printing of "sub-casing" to fasten within PCB enclosure to firmly prevent PCB from vibrating/moving within the enclosure.
